# Practical Guide to Installing and Operating an AODD Pump

**By Antlia Engineering Works — AODD Pump Manufacturer Mumbai, India**

## Installation Steps

1. **Choose Location** — Position pump close to fluid source. Ensure stable, level mounting.
2. **Connect Suction Line** — Use properly sized hose. Apply Teflon tape on threads. Tighten all fittings.
3. **Connect Discharge Line** — Connect to outlet port. Avoid excessively long discharge lines.
4. **Connect Air Supply** — Install FRL unit (Filter Regulator Lubricator) on air inlet.
5. **Start at Low Pressure** — Begin at 2 kg/cm² and gradually increase.
6. **Monitor and Adjust** — Adjust air pressure for required flow rate. Check for leaks.
